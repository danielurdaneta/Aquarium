Aquarium Project

What is the Aquarium Project?


The purpose of this blog is to show how our aquarium evolve over the months and how it went from being a simple fish tank to become a whole ecosystem. Our tank was set up on December of 2020, it is a 10 gallon Waterbox with a Fluval nano light and a Eheim 150W heater.

There have been different plants and fish that have lived in the aquarium , some of them thrived and some of them not, below you can filter month by month using the format "mm/yyyy" to see pictures of the aquarium as well as to see which inhabitants lived in there at that given moment. By default, 12/2020 (the first month) is shown.
